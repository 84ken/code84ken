Origin — Scientific Foundation

The Bouba-Kiki Effect

Humans universally associate round shapes with the sound "bouba"
and angular shapes with "kiki" — across all languages, ages, and cultures.

Wolfgang Köhler, 1929 — Ramachandran & Hubbard, 2001
Translated into brand
